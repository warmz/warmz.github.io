Why doesn’t WarmZ have bed-side controls?

Bed-side controls add cost, increase complexity of setup, and reduce system reliability. The functionality of the bed-side controller can be replaced by a phone app and voice controls. This decision may limit our target market a bit but will reduce costs and simplify setup, guides, troubleshooting, and customer support calls

What are the WarmZ configurations?

WarmZ takes the place of a mattress pad. It has individually controllable temperature areas for body and feet. WarmZ also supports left and right temperature areas. The base unit sits under the head of the bed and contains all of the electronics and power supplies. A single cord connects the pad to the base unit.

What if it breaks?

If you have any issues with your WarmZ, contact us. We will fix it or refund you if it is within the first year.

Can I modify my WarmZ?

Believe it or not. Yeah. Our firmware is open source and we have included a solder bridge for you to crap open and allow for custom firmware. Be aware that doing so is at your own risk and we are not responsible for your irresponsibility. Be smart.

Who is WarmZ for?

Anyone who gets cold at night. But right now people with queen or large sized beds who live in the United States. Sorry other countries, we are working on international certifications and adapters.

Can I machine wash my WarmZ?

Yes. Yes you can. Just make sure to unplug it and not to wash it with the control electronics (the small black box) attached. We've washed ours a few hundred times and so far it's worked out fine.
